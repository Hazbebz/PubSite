About us

Brady's Of Shankill is an Award Winning, Bord Failte accredited pub, restaurant and off licence. Having previously held the prestigious Black & White Pub of the year and voted best bar food and best charity event 2015, Brady's offer award winning breakfast, carvery and A La Carte dining 7 days a week.

Under the direction of leading Dublin publican John Brady since 1992, Brady's Of Shankill is known as one of South Dublin's leading go-to destinations for morning coffee, afternoon tea or evening dining. With a well appointed, heated smoking garden, fully stocked off licence, complimentary Wi-Fi and ample, free car parking.
